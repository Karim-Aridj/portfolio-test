SEO Strategy for Bayzat: Focused Execution & Collaboration

1. Core SEO Framework

Objective: Drive organic growth for Bayzat’s HR SaaS platform through technical optimization, content strategy, and cross-functional collaboration.

Budget: No dedicated SEO budget; relied on existing tools (Ahrefs, GA4, Salesforce).

2. Technical SEO Execution

  • Crawl Efficiency: Identified and resolved 500+ crawl errors (broken links, orphaned pages) using Screaming Frog, improving indexation by 22%.
  • Page Speed: Reduced load times by 40% via image compression and lazy loading.
  • Mobile Optimization: Achieved 95+ Mobile-Friendly scores for core pages.
  • Note: Structured data (schema markup) was not implemented.

3. On-Page & Content Strategy

Keyword Targeting:

  • Focused on high-intent terms like “employee benefits software” and “WPS-compliant payroll UAE.”
  • Localized content for MENA markets (e.g., Arabic guides on UAE labor laws), driving 25% of Gulf-region leads.

Content Architecture:

  • Built 15 pillar pages (e.g., “HR Compliance Guide”) with 50+ supporting blogs, improving internal linking.
  • Result: 113% organic traffic growth and 85 keywords ranking in Google’s top 3.

4. Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)

  • A/B Testing: Partnered on 20+ tests, including:
    • Blog CTAs: Multi-step forms vs. single-step → 50% conversion increase.
    • Landing Page Layouts: Minimalist designs → 25% higher engagement.
  • Retargeting Synergy: Shared high-converting blog headlines with Performance Marketing for Google/LinkedIn Ads, boosting cross-channel ROAS by 20%.
