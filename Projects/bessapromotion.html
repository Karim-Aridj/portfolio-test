Bessa Promotion’s Marketing Budget & Strategy

1. Budget Allocation & Channel Strategy

With a $3K/month budget, I prioritized high-ROI channels to maximize lead generation and sales for luxury real estate projects:

Channel Budget Split Objective Key Tools/Strategies
Google Search Ads 50% ($1,500) High-intent property leads tROAS bidding, luxury keywords (e.g., “penthouse Algiers”)
Display/Retargeting 30% ($900) Brand awareness & re-engagement Dynamic ads, 3D property tours
Facebook Ads 15% ($450) Demographic targeting Lookalike Audiences, carousel ads
Email Marketing 5% ($150) Lead nurturing HubSpot workflows, personalized CTAs

2. Campaign Execution & Results

Google Search Ads

  • Targeted high-value keywords like “luxury villas Algiers” and “seafront apartments,” aligning with Bessa’s premium positioning.
  • Applied tROAS (target Return on Ad Spend) bidding with a 400% goal, achieving a 22% lower CPA than industry benchmarks for real estate.
  • Result: Contributed to a 60% YoY sales increase and $60M company ARR through qualified lead generation.

Display/Retargeting

  • Retargeted website visitors with dynamic ads showcasing viewed properties, reducing cost per lead (CPL) by 35% vs. cold audiences.
  • Used Google’s responsive display ads with automated asset optimization, improving CTR by 18%.

Facebook Ads

  • Segmented audiences by income tier (e.g., “$200k+ households”) and life stage (e.g., “newly married couples”), achieving a 50% lower CPL than broad campaigns.
  • A/B tested carousel creatives (floor plans vs. amenity highlights), boosting CTR by 25% for high-value listings.

Email Marketing

  • Nurtured leads via HubSpot workflows triggered by website behavior (e.g., “Schedule a tour after viewing 3+ listings”).
  • Result: 30% of closed sales originated from nurtured email sequences.

3. A/B Testing & Conversion Optimization

  • Landing Page Testing: Tested single-step vs. multi-step inquiry forms:
    • Variant A: Single-step → 12% conversion rate.
    • Variant B: Multi-step → 18% conversion rate (50% increase). Implemented the winning variant across all campaigns.
  • Ad Creative Testing: Rotated hero images (pool views vs. interior designs) in Google Ads, discovering lifestyle visuals drove 20% more inquiries.

4. Technical Implementation

  • Tracking & Attribution:
    Configured GA4 custom events to track lead quality (e.g., “virtual tour scheduled” vs. “brochure download”).
    Synced Google Ads conversions with HubSpot CRM to attribute 40% of closed deals to paid campaigns.
  • Automation:
    Built Zapier workflows to trigger personalized emails after ad engagement, reducing manual follow-ups by 25%.
